# SSOM Governance (Initial)

SSOM is developed in the open using a lightweight governance model.

## Roles

- **Editor:** Last Mile Engineering Group
- **Contributors:** Open
- **Reviewers:** Invited industry participants

## Decision Process

- Lazy consensus
- Issues and pull requests are the primary discussion mechanism
- Editors resolve deadlock if required

## Evolution

This governance model is expected to evolve as adoption grows.
